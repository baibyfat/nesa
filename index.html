About

Sanitation Solutions Initiative, in partnership with other organizations, public and private sectors, is dedicated to providing and promoting access to sustainable, innovative and affordable sanitation solutions to all communities and public institutions. We provide safe, accessible, sustainable and low cost toilets, cleaning and waste management facilities.

OBJECTIVES:

• Improved Health • Inclusivity • Education • Sustainability

  • Reducing risk of waterborne diseases, through elimination of flies, foul smell and installation of realible cleaning facilities.
  • Enhancing accessible facilities for all, including children and those with disabilities.
  • Empowering people with knowledge about sustainable sanitation practices, hygiene, Safe Toilets Technologie, water sanitation, waste &feacal management practices, maintenance sanitation entrepreneurship.
  • Provide environmentally friendly and low-maintenance sanitation designs.
